## 2.1 ART DIRECTION
    Try to stay as simple as possible. Dont go too all out on details. Were going with ASCII art. It means ENGLISH ONLY.
## 2.2 THEMES
    Themes will work as following. They are interchangeble. That means. When you delete one. it should not destroy the site.
    For this to apply. The css must be simple. For themes. well they are just colors.
        THE CLASSES TO BE USED.
            GREEN        -  GREEN COLOR
            YELLOW       -  YELLOW COLOR
            BLUE         -  BLUE COLOR
            RED          -  RED COLOR
            WHITE        -  SHADE OF WHITE
            BACKGROUND   -  BACKGROUND
            ERROR        -  RED BOLD
            WARNING      -  YELLOW OR ORANGE AND BOLD
            SUCCESS      -  GREEN BOLD
    Themes go into the css theme folder.
## 2.3 Way to switch the themes. 
Themes are switchable by a command named. COLOR
## 2.4 What to store inside site.css
    Positioning of objects. Their size. Borders etc. DO NOT STORE ANYTHING THAT ADDS SOMETHING VISUAL. LIKE BODER COLOR!!!
